Promoting Peace and Order

Crimes, violence, disturbance and not good sights are unavoidable in the society. In order to minimize the occurrence of such crimes, violence, etc., maintainance of peace and order is an essential part of the community. This is where we come in, the peace and order committee headed by the barangay captain. Peace and order in the country is necessary for progress and it should also start in the barangay.
